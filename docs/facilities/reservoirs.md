# Supply Reservoirs

The City of Albany draws water from two primary upland reservoirs located in the Helderberg Mountains.

## 1. Alcove Reservoir
* **Type:** Primary Supply.
* **Capacity:** ~12 Billion Gallons.
* **Characteristics:** High quality, low turbidity water.
* **Intake:** Water flows by gravity to the Filtration Plant.

## 2. Basic Creek Reservoir
* **Type:** Secondary / Feeder Supply.
* **Function:** Flows into Alcove Reservoir via a connecting tunnel.
* **Management:** Used to top off Alcove during dry seasons.

!!! note "Watershed Protection"
    Boating, swimming, and unauthorized fishing are strictly prohibited to maintain raw water quality.
