# Sodium Permanganate


## Sodium Permanganate (NaMnO4)
* **Purpose:** Taste & Odor control; Iron/Manganese removal.
* **Appearance:** Deep purple liquid.
* **Staining:** Will permanently stain skin and concrete brown/black.
* **Overdose Sign:** If finished water has a **pink tint**, permanganate is passing through the filters. **Reduce dose immediately.**
